# Langfuse

## What It Is

Langfuse is an open-source observability and evaluation platform for LLM applications. It provides tracing, metrics, and evaluation capabilities similar to [[entities/langsmith]] but with the advantage of being self-hostable.

## Key Details

- Open-source alternative to [[entities/langsmith]]
- Recommended for Project 3 (Monitoring & Observability) in AI Projects 2026
- Tracks SRE-style metrics: P50/P95 latency, cost per request, failure rates

## How It Fits In

- Part of the [[concepts/observability]] layer in the [[concepts/ai-engineering-stack]]
- Alternative to [[entities/langsmith]] and Braintrust for AI system monitoring
- Used alongside [[entities/langchain]] or independently
